Our Products

We have items from historic buildings for sale that will add a vintage architectural touch to your home.

There are lots of vintage kitchen items such as rolling pins, cake pans and a wide variety of Pyrex.

Little trinkets abound and make for great gifts! There is something for everyone here.

Our selection of primitive wares is extensive and are some of our bestsellers!

We even have spooky and offbeat vintage items to make your house Halloween-ready.

Papercrafts and postcards are also a big draw and we love looking at vintage artwork.

Santas and snowmen and all kinds of Christmas decorations abound - these are some of our favorite things to buy!

Seriously, we love the holidays and there is always themed decor for every holiday on your list.

Who is Not My Sister's Style?

Not My Sister's Style is what happened when two of the three Bowman sisters, Marie and Jenny, decided that after years of attending auctions and estate sales they should start selling what they were buying. Marie would often send pictures of her unique finds to Jenny, to which Jenny would usually respond, "That's not my style."

Marie

The older sister. Creative, always on-the-go, but never punctual.

Jenny

The younger sister. The brains behind the operation, practical, usually punctual.
